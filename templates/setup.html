Zero-Knowledge Private Security

Unlike online web tools, Award Tracker **never** stores your logins or credentials on a cloud server.

All passwords are encrypted directly inside a local SQLite database using **industry-grade AES-256 (Fernet) local cryptography**. The decryption key is derived solely from your local Master Password.

IMPORTANT: Because encryption is 100% local, there is no "Forgot Password" or "Reset Link" option. Store your Master Password securely.
